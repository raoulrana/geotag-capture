Privacy Policy — GeoTag Capture

Last updated: July 11, 2026

GeoTag Capture ("the app") is a camera app that stamps location, address, time, and an optional map onto photos you take. This page explains what data the app accesses and how it is used.

Data the app accesses

  • Camera — used only to show the live viewfinder and capture photos. Photos never leave your device except when you explicitly save or share them.
  • Location (GPS) — used to stamp coordinates onto each photo, to look up a human-readable address, to fetch local weather at capture time, and to draw a small map thumbnail. Location is read while the app is open and is not collected, stored, or transmitted by us in the background.
  • Photo library — the app requests permission to save the finished, geotagged photo to your device's photo library. It does not read or access your existing photos.

What we don't do

  • No account or sign-in is required or collected.
  • No analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs are included.
  • We do not operate a server, and captured photos and their metadata are never uploaded to us. All captures are stored locally on your device (in on-device storage) until you delete them or uninstall the app.
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties for advertising.

Third-party services

To turn your coordinates into an address, weather reading, and map image, the app sends your current latitude/longitude directly from your device to these public services at the moment you capture or preview a location — not through any server we operate:

  • OpenStreetMap Nominatim — reverse geocoding (coordinates → address)
  • Open-Meteo — current weather conditions
  • OpenStreetMap tile servers — map thumbnail images

Each of these services may log the coordinates and standard request metadata (e.g. IP address) it receives, under its own privacy policy. We do not control or have access to those logs.

Data retention and deletion

Captured photos and their metadata are stored only on your device. You can delete individual captures from the app's history view, or remove all app data by deleting the app.

Children's privacy

GeoTag Capture is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the "Last updated" date above will be revised.
